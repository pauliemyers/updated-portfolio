PAUL ARTHUR MYERS

A portfolio of digital media

Paul is a student of Integrated Digital Media at NYU Tandon School of Engineering. He transferred to NYU from the University of Connecticut's School of Fine arts, has studied at the University of Wollongong in Australian, has attended GSummit and Exploring Future Reality conferences, and took Dr. B.J Fogg's Behavior Design master class.

He is focusing his education and senior thesis on the health and behavioral applications of virtual reality and serious games. As a designer of digital media, Paul's goal is to create content to help improve peoples' lives and to create queer-friendly and queer-inclusive content and narratives.

He is a gamer, a musician, a music arranger, a student, a birdwatcher, a twin, a brother, a son, and a friend.
